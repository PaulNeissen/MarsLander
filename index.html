Welcome to the Mars lander simulation 1.0, the goal here is to land the pod on the flat ground. Use the left and right keys to tangle the pod and the up and down keys to tilt the pod.
The zone is 7000m wide and 3000m high, the origin (0,0) being the bottom left corner.
